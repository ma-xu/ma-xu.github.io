About

I am an Applied Research Scientist at Amazon AGI Foundation, working on the Nova 2 project, focusing on multimodal and visual generation.

I received my Ph.D. from the College of Engineering at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun Raymond Fu in the SMILE Lab. Before that, I spent two years in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of North Texas, working with Prof. Song Fu. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Nanjing Forestry University, China.

My research interests include Model Efficiency Multimodal LLMs Generative AI Visual Generation

I did internships at Bell Labs eBay Microsoft Adobe Meta GenAI Thanks to all my mentors and collaborators for the guidance and help, always appreciate.
